longer bio

Charles Brian Quinn, or CBQ, is the co-founder and CEO of Greenzie, creator of software for autonomous robotic lawn mowers: a venture-backed company in the Atlanta Ventures Studio. He is also a managing member of Kelp Engine LLC, an advisory and investment firm for technology-backed companies. He is a co-owner and serves on the board of Big Nerd Ranch LLC. Big Nerd Ranch teaches immersive software development bootcamp courses, writes best-selling books, and builds digital products for companies of all sizes. CBQ has experience in venture-backed startups such as CloudTags (as CTO), and VestMe (as CTO of a spin-off of Holcombe Financial LLC), in addition to Private Equity backed companies such as lawn.com. He was formerly CEO of Big Nerd Ranch for 2 years. Before that, he was co-founder and CEO of Highgroove Studios, a hyperspecialty consulting firm that built back-ends and APIs to web and mobile apps. He has co-founded, created, and exited several technology and service companies including ScoutApp and Slingshot Hosting. He is a software developer, entrepreneur, investor, author, speaker, self-proclaimed coffee snob, and he is always up for a run.


He is a member of the Georgia Tech Computing Alumni Affinity Group, a member of the Young Presidents' Organization and the YPO Southern 7 Chapter. He is a former member and Board Member of Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Atlanta, a graduate and former chairman of the Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Accelerator Program in Atlanta. He serves as a mentor in the Georgia Tech Mentor Jackets Program, and is an amateur sailor and member of the Lake Lanier Association and the Atlanta Track Club.


He lives with his wife, Katie, and daughter Marcie, in midtown, Atlanta, Georgia.

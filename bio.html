Biographical Information for Charles Brian Quinn

bio

Charles Brian Quinn is the managing member of Kelp Engine LLC, an advisory services company to technology firms. In this role he serves as an advisor, board member, and fractional CTO. He is a co-owner and a Principal at Big Nerd Ranch LLC, formed via merger in 2012. Big Nerd Ranch teaches immersive app development bootcamp courses, writes best-selling books, and builds apps for companies of all sizes. CBQ, as he is affectionately known, has experience in venture-backed startups such as CloudTags (as CTO), and VestMe (as CTO of a spin-off of Holcombe Financial LLC), in addition to Private Equity backed companies such as lawn.com. He was formerly CEO of Big Nerd Ranch for 2 years. Before that, he was co-founder and CEO of Highgroove Studios, a hyperspecialty consulting firm that built back-ends and APIs to web and mobile apps. He has co-founded and started several technology and service companies including Scout and Slingshot Hosting. He is a software developer, entrepreneur, investor, author, speaker, self-proclaimed coffee snob, and he enjoys competitive sailing and racing two-wheeled vehicles in the mud and on the track.


He is a member of the Georgia Tech Computing Alumni Organization, a member of the Young Presidents' Organization and the YPO Southern 7 Chapter. He is a former member and Board Member of Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Atlanta, a graduate and former chairman of the Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Accelerator Program in Atlanta. He serves as a mentor in the Georgia Tech Mentor Jackets Program, and is an amateur sailor and member of the Lake Lanier Association.


He lives with his wife, Katie, in midtown, Atlanta, Georgia.

Charles Brian Quinn

strengths

I am competitive, motivated, and a self-starter. I am entrepreneurial (enjoy starting things up) and like maintaining control over projects and leading the charge, or as my wife puts it: “in any group, people tend to look to you for guidance and leadership — others ask you to take charge and enjoy your direction". I am positive, optimistic, and find energy in leading others. I’m confident and able to manipulate people (in a positive way), in other words: persuasive and able to inspire others to reach their maximum potential even in hostile situations. I have high operational goals for myself and others and expect all involved to provide 100% effort. I’m adaptive and open to new ideas and change: I will react, adjust, and modify behavior in a variety of situations. I need variety in projects, environment (freedom to move around in office or around country), and groups/teams (to get and maintain attention of networks and groups) to be effective.


I work towards making things happen (bias towards action), I can juggle many projects and activities simultaneously. I demand high performance of self and others on my teams. I am a great presenter: engaging and skilled at verbal expression, and can deliver messages confidently: persuasively, or authoritatively if needed. I am able to build trust, credibility, and rapport. I am able to make decisions quickly and take the credit or blame for the outcome of decisions.


I am seeking a strong, visible group or organization with which to identify. In my most recent roles, I have either created or co-created the culture, brand, values, and/or mission of which I become a fierce defender and promoter. I am great at hiring, mentoring, managing, and giving constructive feedback: managing up and down to the results that really matter.


I love technology, software, and new, innovative ideas and concepts. Yes, I will be the first with the cybernetic implant in my skull, and yes, I own Bitcoin.


weaknesses

I regularly maintain a sense of urgency that can be perceived as impatience. I have been known to become easily bored by mundane or routine projects, even with my ability to inject creativity and spontaneity to the tasks at hand. I can lose interest in a project or initiative once the challenge is gone and it becomes more of a routine. I can become restless and impatient for those who do not share my vision, overly complex processes, and slow-moving work. I need a system of support for follow-through and loose-ends. I often set overly ambitious, somewhat-unrealistic expectations and can come across a bit aggressive and dominating, only hearing what I want to hear at times.

seeking

I am looking to start or run a company. I am open to running a larger division/area of a company given enough leadership and P&L responsibility. While I love the true early stage startup, and self-identify as entrepreneurial ("if it doesn't exist, I will create it"), some of my previous successes have not been in the "let's buy the domain and setup the email accounts" stage. I am also open to acquiring a company, with our without some private equity backing.

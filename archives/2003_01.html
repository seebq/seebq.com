the new site

All I can say is, it's been about time.

seebq.com is back in action, and this time, better than ever. The new server is amazingly tight and with movable type's fully customizable software, a fresh new linux install, a gigabit connection to the net, and lots of late night hacking, I couldn't be more happy with the result.

The site is, as always, a work in progress, but now, that work is a little bit easier. I know, I know, I'm jumping onto the weblog bandwagon. And just as wired magazine moved it from "wired" to "tired" on the trends list. But, not to worry folks, seebq.com is on the cutting edge of technology, and rest assured we'll be diving head first into the next big internet trend sooner than you can say interactive-cybernetic-duck-orgy.

Stay tuned for lots of fresh updates, phat pics, and more of the good stuff.

Charles Brian Quinn

strengths

I am competitive, motivated, and a self-starter. I am entrepreneurial (enjoy starting things up) and enjoy maintaining control over projects and leading the charge, or as my wonderful wife puts it: “in any group, people tend to look to you for guidance and leadership — others ask you to take charge and enjoy your direction." I am always positive, optimistic, and find energy in leading others. I am confident and able to persuade people to join the cause, and able to inspire others to reach their maximum potential even in challenging situations. I have high operational goals for myself and others and expect all involved to provide 100% effort. I’m adaptive and open to new ideas and change: I will react, adjust, and modify behavior in a variety of situations. I need variety in projects, environment (freedom to move around in office or new locations), and groups/teams to be most effective.


I work towards making things happen (bias towards action). I can juggle many projects and activities simultaneously. I demand high performance of my self and others on my teams. I am a great presenter: engaging and skilled at verbal expression, and can deliver messages confidently: persuasively, or authoritatively if needed. I am able to build trust, credibility, and rapport. I am able to make decisions quickly and like sharing the credit and accepting the blame for the outcome of decisions.


I am seeking a strong, visible group or organization with which to identify. In my most recent roles, I have either created or co-created the culture, brand, values, and/or mission of which I become a fierce defender and promoter. I am great at hiring, mentoring, managing, and giving constructive feedback: managing up and down to the results that really matter.


I love technology, software, and new, innovative ideas and concepts. Yes, I will be the first to signup for the cybernetic implant in my skull, and yes, I own cryptocurrency, stream my VR rig gaming on Twitch, and chances are good that I've read that sci-fi book.


weaknesses

I regularly maintain a sense of urgency that can be perceived as impatience. I have been known to become easily bored by mundane or routine projects, even with my ability to inject creativity and spontaneity to the tasks at hand. I can lose interest in a project or initiative once the challenge is gone and it becomes more of a routine. I can become restless and impatient for those who do not share my vision, overly complex processes, and slow-moving work. I need a system of support for follow-through and loose-ends. I often set overly ambitious, somewhat-unrealistic expectations and can come across a bit aggressive and dominating, only hearing what I want to hear at times.

seeking

I am looking to start, run, or lead a division/area of an organization with real P&L responsibility. While I love the true early stage startup, and self-identify as entrepreneurial ("that needs to exist, let's create it"), most of my previous successes have not been in the very early "let's buy the domain and setup the email accounts" stage, but I can do that too.

Biographical Information for Charles Brian Quinn

bio

Charles Brian Quinn is a co-owner and co-founder of Big Nerd Ranch LLC, formed via merger in 2012. Big Nerd Ranch teaches immersive app development bootcamp courses, writes best-selling books, and builds apps for companies of all sizes. CBQ, as he is affectionately known, served as CEO for 2 years (December 2014) and now serves on the board. Before that, he was co-founder and CEO of Highgroove Studios, a hyperspecialty consulting firm that built back-ends and APIs to mobile apps. He has co-founded and started several technology and service companies including Scout and Slingshot Hosting. He is a software developer, entrepreneur, investor, author, speaker, self-proclaimed coffee snob, and he enjoys sailing and racing two-wheeled vehicles in the mud and on the track.


He is the Chairman of the Georgia Tech Computing Alumni Organization, a member of the Young Presidents' Organization and the YPO Southern 7 Chapter. He is a member and former Board Member of Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Atlanta, a graduate and former chairman of the Entrepreneur's Organization (EO) Accelerator Program in Atlanta. He serves as a mentor in the Georgia Tech Mentor Jackets Program, is an Ambassador to TechBridge, and is an amateur sailor and member of the Lake Lanier Association.


He lives with his fiance, Katie, in midtown, Atlanta, Georgia.
